More Than Just Paper. It's a Craft.


The act of putting pen to paper is a profound form of connection, creativity, and mindfulness. Our mission is to provide
the tools that transform everyday writing into a moment of intention and beauty.

Story: From a Simple Love of Ink

ALBERT's STATIONARY began not in a boardroom, but at a dusty antique desk. Our founder, ALBERT,
spent years collecting vintage stationery, fascinated by the stories held in the texture of paper and the flow of ink.
She noticed that modern notebooks and pens often felt disposable, lacking the soul and quality to inspire. In 2015,
driven by a passion for craftsmanship, ALBERT started binding her own journals and sourcing fine writing instruments.
What began as a hobby for friends quickly grew into a community of fellow stationery lovers.
Today, ALBERT's Stationary is a dedicated team of designers, artisans, and stationery enthusiasts, united by a shared belief:
that the tools you use should elevate your ideas and celebrate your voice.

Our Philosophy:

1. Intentional Design:

Every product we create is thoughtfully designed to be both beautiful and functional. From the lie-flat binding of our notebooks
to the perfectly balanced weight of our pens, every detail is considered to enhance your writing experience.

3. Lasting Quality:

We reject the culture of disposability. Our products are built to last, to become worn and loved, to be the constant companion
